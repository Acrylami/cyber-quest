Password Cracking

Manual Cracking

100 points

When you think of hacking, one of the first things that might come into your mind is the act of password cracking. Put simply this is trying to login to an account by guessing the password. This is usually done with tools, but for this training we will be doing it manually.

Did you know that the top 25 most used passwords make up 10% of all passwords? That means if you tried every one of those 25 passwords on any account, you would have a 1 in 10 chance of hacking into the account. Scary stuff right?
