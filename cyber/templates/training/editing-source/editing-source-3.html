The hidden attribute is responsible for hiding whatever tag its placed in. It could be used to hide an image, form field, or in this case a button. To make a hidden tag display again, you simply need to remove the hidden attribute.

On the last page we managed to remove the disabled attribute from our button by inspecting the source code, selecting the disabled word, and deleting it. The hidden attribute is no different
