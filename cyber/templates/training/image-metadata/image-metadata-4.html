In image metadata, the location is given in the form of longitude and latitude coordinates. This will appear in one of the following formats:

51.482159, -3.181145 51.482159 N, 3.181145 W 51°28'55.8"N 3°10'52.1"W

This system can describe any point on the planet with two numbers: The first being longitude (up and down), the second being latitude (left and right)

If you plug this into Google Maps, you'll be able to see that the coordinates above all pinpoint Cardiff Castle
